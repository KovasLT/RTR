          Platform Core Mission

          RTR (Rising Teams Rating) provides data transparency and structural clarity for grassroots competitive teams. By implementing centralized regional indicators, our goal is to highlight rising organizational talent, offering direct scouting conduits for national squads, federations, and major professional tier scouts looking across localized borders.

          Algorithmic Methodology

          Rankings use custom points distribution protocols weighted against parameters containing tournament tier importance, victory margins, match recency, and specific historical rival metrics.

          Inactivity Decay Clause: Active calculations expire automatically. Teams remaining unplayed across an interval larger than 30 consecutive calendar tracking days suffer progressive automatic point decay parameters to secure live ladder validation.
